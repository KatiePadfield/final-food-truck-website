Our Menu

Our menu changes throughout the day to provide you with best experience during the day, or night, no matter the time. We can provide you with a range of coffee’s, tea’s as well as freshly baked goods everyday up until 5pm when we switch across to our evening menu “Dusk” to serve you delicious cocktails, fun and flavourful mocktails as well as stocking a wide selection of gin. If you have any dietary requirements, please do let us know so we can provide you with the best service possible. If you also take a look at our menu, you will see we have labelled foods which are vegan and gluten free to ease your decision making.
